Become a Member!

Future Applicants

If you are interested in joining GUARD Dogs we are always accepting new members.

Please consider filling out our application and we will review it as soon as possible. You will receive an email from us within the next week (or so).


MEMBER REQUIREMENTS:

Joining GUARD Dogs is an excellent way to meet new people, help out your fellow students, and have fun! Member jobs include working in the dispatch office, driving, and riding-along. A number of leadership positions are also available for members who want to become more involved.

GUARD Dogs members will be required to work at least 2 nights per semester.

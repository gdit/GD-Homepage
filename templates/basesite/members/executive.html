Executive Board

The backbone of GUARD Dogs consists of an elite team of individuals who strive to uphold the values of this organization. The Executive Board is responsible for all administrative and managerial tasks necessary for the upkeep, future development, and present operation of GUARD Dogs. Three directors are elected to serve for a fiscal year, beginning in May of each year. To assist with their duties, officers are appointed to specific position on the Board. A dedicated team of General Executive Board Members also serve, working collaboratively with the officers and directors, to promote the organization's stature. If you are interested in becoming a larger part of this organization, please contact us.

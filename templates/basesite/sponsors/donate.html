Non-profit Donations

For your connivance, we hold an account with the University of Connecticut Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. This allows you to collect a federal tax deduction for your donation. For more information about placing a donation, you can review our donation and sponsorship packet or contact us, we would be glad to work with you and answer any questions you may have.

Checks should be made out to The University of Connecticut Foundation, Inc with GUARD Dogs listed in the memo section.

Checks can be mailed to the following address:

Storrs, CT 06269-3008

Operation

GUARD Dogs operates Friday and Saturday nights from 11:00pm to 3:00am. All services are free but donations are encouraged as operating a free service has many expenses. Rides are provided on a first come first serve basis and no preference or discrimination will be given.

Call Us

During our operating hours dial (860) 486-8000 to reach our dispatch.

Apartment Complexes

GUARD Dogs will pick up and bring students home to their on or off-campus residences, given that they are within a 4 mile radius of campus. The following is a list of common pick-up / drop-off locations that we will go to. These are only a few of our common pick-up / drop-off locations and we will go to others given that they are within a 4 mile radius of campus.
